Prediction 5: Shift towards topic clusters and pillar pages


As we look ahead to the future of SEO in the 80920 area, one major trend that we can expect to see is a shift towards topic clusters and pillar pages. This strategy involves organizing content around specific topics, with a pillar page serving as the main hub of information, and supporting content (or cluster content) linking back to the pillar page.

This approach not only helps to improve the user experience by providing more comprehensive information on a particular topic, but it also aligns well with search engine algorithms that are increasingly prioritizing topic relevance and authority. By creating a network of interlinked content that covers a topic from multiple angles, businesses can improve their chances of ranking well in search results.
